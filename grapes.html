Citrus

Delassus Group manages 2 500 ha of own production and exports up to 40 000 t per year of citrus fruits. Delassus invested in state of art pack house fully equipped and certified to pack customers’ brands as well as Delassus own brands.

NEW! Leanri ®

Delassus cooperates with BioGold (South Africa) on new varieties of citrus. Delassus launched the new easy peeler, extra juicy, extra tasty Leanri®. This hybrid cultivar (clementine x mandarin) has a deep-orange, fairly thin rind with relatively low oil content and is very easy to peel. In Morocco, it is available from early November.

Delassus monitors and advises producers affiliated to the Group. It provides the necessary support to assist them in upgrading their quality.

8 types of citrus

Clementine: Nules type and Nour are available from October to January

Mandarina: Nadorcott and Leanri ® are available from November to April

Navels: Navel type, and Navel lane late are available from December to April

Oranges: Salustiana and Maroc late are available from January to July.
